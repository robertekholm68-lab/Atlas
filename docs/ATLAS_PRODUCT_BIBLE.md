# ATLAS Product Bible

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Foundation Draft  
**Project:** ATLAS  
**Platform Engine:** Fitness OS

---

## Executive Summary

ATLAS is an AI-powered fitness platform built around a single core principle:

> **The human body should become the primary interface.**

Rather than presenting users with disconnected statistics, charts and logs, ATLAS creates a living digital representation of the body that continuously evolves based on training, recovery, nutrition, sleep, body composition and long-term goals.

ATLAS is designed to simplify decision-making rather than increase data consumption.

Every recommendation should answer one question:

> **What should I do next?**

ATLAS combines sports science, wearable technology, artificial intelligence and modern software design into one unified system.

---

## Vision

To build the world's most intelligent Digital Human Twin for health, fitness and performance.

ATLAS should become the operating system for human physical development.

Instead of tracking isolated activities, it should understand the relationship between every input and every outcome:

- Training
- Recovery
- Nutrition
- Sleep
- Stress
- Consistency
- Goals

Everything influences everything else.

ATLAS exists to make those relationships visible.

## Product Identity

ATLAS is not:

- A basic workout log
- A calorie counter
- A wearable dashboard
- A bodybuilding app
- A collection of graphs

ATLAS is:

- A Digital Human Twin
- A performance operating system
- An intelligent decision-support platform
- A long-term training companion
- A visual representation of human progress

---

## Product Philosophy

### The Body Is the Interface

The anatomical body is the primary dashboard.

Charts support the body. Statistics support the body. The user should understand their current physical status simply by looking at the Body Map.

### Simplicity Creates Motivation

Users lose motivation when they are overwhelmed.

ATLAS transforms advanced physiology into intuitive visual feedback.

### Every Recommendation Must Explain Why

Recommendations without reasoning create distrust.

ATLAS must always explain why a recommendation is made.

### Long-Term Progress Wins

ATLAS optimizes for sustainable progress rather than short-term intensity.

### Every User Is Unique

Recovery, adaptation, motivation and nutrition differ between users.

ATLAS should learn each individual over time.

## Core Platform Modules

### Mission Control

The home screen and command center. It answers what the user should do today.

### Body Map

The primary interface: an anatomical Digital Human Twin with anterior and posterior views.

### Muscle Cards

Detailed muscle-level insight panels accessed from the Body Map.

### Body Layers

Visual modes for Recovery, Training, Goals, Balance and Performance.

### Workout Builder

Creates, logs and completes workouts while feeding structured data into the Digital Human Twin.

### Exercise Library

The authoritative source for exercise definitions, movement patterns and muscle activation profiles.

### Recovery Dashboard

A dedicated view for whole-body, system and muscle-specific recovery.

### Goals Dashboard

The operational interface for Goal Intelligence, forecasts and trajectory tracking.

### AI Coach Interface

The conversational layer that explains insights and recommendations.

### Nutrition Dashboard

Tracks food, macros, water and nutrition adherence.

### Wearables & Integrations

Imports external physiological data from Garmin, Apple Health, Health Connect, Oura, WHOOP and related platforms.

### User Profile & Settings

Maintains identity, preferences, baseline values, notifications, privacy and accessibility.

## Fitness Intelligence Engine

The Fitness Intelligence Engine is the central decision engine of ATLAS.

It transforms raw user data into:

- Muscle activation
- Recovery state
- Readiness
- Goal trajectory
- Forecasts
- Recommendations
- AI explanations

It consists of:

- Muscle Intelligence
- Recovery Engine
- Goal Intelligence
- Prediction Engine
- Recommendation Engine
- AI Coach

No interface module performs physiological calculations directly.

---

## The Body Map

The Body Map is not an illustration.

It is the primary operating interface.

Requirements:

- Skin removed
- Superficial muscles visible
- Anterior and posterior views
- Every visible muscle addressable
- Default grey-red desaturated base
- Layer-based visual presentation
- Mobile and desktop support
- Muscle selection and Muscle Cards

---

## Mission Control

Mission Control is the first screen after login.

It should answer within five seconds:

- How am I today?
- What should I train?
- Am I recovering?
- Am I progressing?
- What should I do next?

Mission Control prioritizes action over statistics.

---

## AI Coach

AI Coach is the voice of ATLAS.

It converts calculations into natural language.

Every AI message should include:

1. Observation
2. Explanation
3. Recommendation
4. Expected outcome

The tone is calm, professional, encouraging and never judgmental.

---

## Product Success Criteria

ATLAS succeeds when users:

- Understand their body more clearly
- Make better training decisions
- Recover more effectively
- Stay consistent longer
- Reach goals with greater confidence
- Open the app because it provides clarity

---

## MVP Definition

The first public version should include:

- Authentication
- Mission Control
- Body Map
- Muscle Cards
- Body Layers
- Workout Builder
- Exercise Library
- Recovery Dashboard
- Goals Dashboard
- AI Coach Interface
- Nutrition Dashboard
- User Profile & Settings

Wearable integrations may be phased depending on implementation complexity.

---

## Product Rule

Every future feature must answer:

> **Does this help the user understand their body better?**

If not, it does not belong in ATLAS.
